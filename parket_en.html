NOBILWOOD PRODUCES HIGH-QUALITY NATURAL WOOD PARQUET
FOR YOUR ELEGANT HOME


Excellent stability thanks to the high-quality wear layer.
Our parquet is specially designed for spaces with underfloor heating, featuring a triple-layered structure that includes a 3.5 mm noble top layer of oak, an 8 mm middle layer of spruce, and a 2.5 mm base layer of spruce for added stability.

With a total thickness of 14 mm, NOBILWOOD parquet ensures resistance to temperature and humidity variations. Investing in laminated parquet is a smart choice for a durable and high-quality flooring solution that will last for many years. It is easy to install thanks to the interlocking system and allows for quick and hassle-free interior updates in your home.

Dare to be different and choose NOBILWOOD wood parquet for your unique and individual flooring.
