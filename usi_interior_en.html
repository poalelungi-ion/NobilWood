DISCOVER THE PREMIUM DOORS FROM NOBILWOOD


Interior doors are one of the most important purchases for homes, commercial buildings, and offices, combining value that can be identified with tradition, craftsmanship, skill, and natural beauty. The role of doors in interior design is to complement the decorative style or create a strong contrast with the interior design, which can be achieved through a variety of colors and models.


We design and manufacture the most high-performing interior doors on the market, focusing on the quality of materials and technological innovation. We offer a wide range of options for customizing your desired product.

Explore the elegance and innovation of modern doors from NOBIL WOOD!

  • Door Types: Modern, Classic, Neoclassic
  • Cladding: Wood, Glass, HPL, Stone, Ceramic
  • Structure: Oak laminated wood + Reinforcement profile + Sound insulation substrate
  • Hardware: AGB Italy
  • Wood Finish: Opaque or Transparent, Renner, Italy
  • Dimensions: Max. H 3000
