TRANSFORM YOUR HOME INTERIOR
CONNECTED WITH NATURE


NOBIL WOOD sliding systems are the ideal solution for large openings, creating a smooth transition between interior and exterior, thus enhancing creative possibilities and design solutions.
With a simple lift and slide motion, these doors transform any space, offering easy and elegant access.

Sliding systems are often chosen for projects where perfect architectural integration is desired, with nearly invisible transitions between exterior and interior.
Sliding systems can be made from wood or wood clad with aluminum, offering adaptable variants for different architectural requirements.

  • Laminated wood: Oak, Ash, Pine, Larch, Walnut
  • Profile: total 192 mm
  • Glass: 24 – 50 mm
  • Fittings: AGB Italy with lift, 3 seals
  • Aluminum: Aluminum cladding Italy
  • Wood finish: 4 layers, water-based lacquer Renner Italy + logo colors
  • Aluminum finish: Electrostatic painting, Italy + logo colors
  • UW value: 1.1 W/m2k
