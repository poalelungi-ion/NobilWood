DISCOVER DURABILITY, ENERGY EFFICIENCY
and UNMATCHED COMFORT with NOBILWOOD WINDOWS


Wood is one of the oldest materials used by humans in construction. Thus, today, wood lends a classic and noble air to any project.
Wood & aluminum windows combine the beauty of wood on the interior with the durability of aluminum on the exterior, offering an elegant and weather-resistant solution.

Aluminum-clad wood windows represent the highest technological level of windows. This combination provides superior thermal insulation and a pleasant appearance, seamlessly integrating into the modern or traditional architecture of any space. Wood & aluminum windows are the optimal choice when we want to enjoy the beauty of wood without worrying about how it will withstand external weather conditions over time.

BGS Nobil System

A minimalist design window, with only a single aluminum frame visible on the exterior. Wood-aluminum windows combine the beauty of wood on the interior with the durability of aluminum on the exterior, offering an elegant and weather-resistant solution. The exterior aluminum guarantees high weather resistance, ensuring the windows will look great even after 50 years.


MATERIALS and HARDWARE:
  • Laminated wood: Oak, Ash, Pine, Larch, Walnut
  • Profile: 90 mm
  • Glass: 24 – 50 mm
  • Hardware: AGB Italy, 4 seals
  • Aluminum: Aluminum cladding Italy
  • Wood finish: 4 layers, water-based varnish Renner Italy
  • Aluminum finish: Electrostatic powder coating, Italy
  • UW value: 1.1 W/m²K

Nobil Therm System

Energy efficiency and sustainability are becoming increasingly important and mandatory according to global construction standards. A Nobil Therm window from NOBIL WOOD meets the highest standards, qualifying for passive house certification. Featuring a three-layer system: laminated wood + thermal module + welded aluminum, combined with high-performance glass, this window achieves Uw coefficients of 0.75 W/m²K.


MATERIALS and HARDWARE:
  • Laminated wood: Oak, Ash, Pine, Larch, Walnut
